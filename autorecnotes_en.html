AutoRecNotes

With this application, I'm trying to cover a functionality that I was missing, and that was available at the HTC P3300.

The idea is to record short audio notes quickly, using the fewest possible steps to begin recording.

  • In this application we set the duration of recording, which comprises from 10 to 40 seconds.
  • Automatically record whenever start, can be set.
  • Once it starts to record, doesn't mind if the device screen turns off, it'll be recording during the configured time
  • Manage Recorded Notes allows to: play, share by email, rename and delete.
  • Recorded Notes will be stored at external storage, at AutoRecNotes folder.
  • It's highly recommended to create a shortcut at Home Screen so can get a quickly access to application.
